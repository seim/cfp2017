> Saint-Petersburg, Russia _ April 21, 2017
The Second Conference on Software Engineering and Information Management SEIM-2017 aims to bring together students, researchers and practitioners in different areas of software engineering and information management. We consider SEIM-2017 to be a stepping stone for young researchers, which should help them familiarize with the conference workflow, practice writing academic papers, gather valuable feedback about their research and expand their research network.

Submissions

Papers must be submitted electronically, via the SEIM-2017 EasyChair website.

All submissions must come in PDF format, conform to the IEEE eXpress Conference Publishing template, and not exceed 5 pages (including figures, appendices and references). Both Russian and English papers are welcome.

SEIM-2017 accepts two kinds of submissions: regular and echo. Regular papers must describe original work which has not been published or is not currently submitted elsewhere, and will appear in the conference proceedings if accepted. Echo papers may describe work which has been already published, but they will not be published in the proceedings.

Depending on the number and quality of submissions, the Program Committee may present the best papers with the Distinguished Paper award. These papers will be considered for publication in one of the peer-reviewed Russian journals, after passing an additional round of reviews.

Reviewing

SEIM-2017 will pursue a single-blind review process. To be eligible for publication in the conference proceedings, the authors must account for the reviewer’s comments and questions in the camera-ready version of the paper to the best of their abilities.

Proceedings

СConference proceedings will be submitted for indexation to the Russian Science Citation Index. Additionally, selected papers will be submitted to CEUR-WS.org for online publication.
